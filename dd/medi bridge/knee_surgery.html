Total Knee Replacement

A comprehensive procedure to replace the damaged knee joint with an artificial one, restoring function and relieving pain.

More details

Partial Knee Replacement

Only the damaged portion of the knee joint is replaced, preserving healthy bone and tissue.

Arthroscopic Knee Surgery

A minimally invasive procedure used to diagnose and treat knee issues using small incisions and a camera.

Cartilage Repair Surgery

A specialized procedure aimed at restoring damaged cartilage and preventing further joint degeneration.

ACL/PCL Reconstruction

Reconstruction of torn ligaments in the knee to restore stability and prevent further injury.

Revision Knee Replacement

Replacement of a previously implanted knee joint due to wear, loosening, or infection.

Knee Osteotomy

A procedure that involves reshaping the bones to relieve pressure on the knee joint.

Meniscus Repair Surgery

Repairing a torn meniscus to restore knee function and reduce pain.

Knee Fracture Surgery

A surgical procedure to treat fractures in the bones forming the knee joint.

Patella Realignment Surgery

A procedure to correct dislocation or misalignment of the kneecap.
