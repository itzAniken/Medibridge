About Apollo Hospital

Apollo Hospital, New Delhi, is one of India's most prestigious healthcare institutions, offering state-of-the-art medical facilities and highly qualified professionals. Known for its exceptional patient care, Apollo has been a trusted name in the healthcare sector for decades.

Specialties

  • Cardiology and Cardiothoracic Surgery
  • Neurology and Neurosurgery
  • Orthopedics and Joint Replacement
  • Oncology
  • Gastroenterology
  • Transplant Services (Liver, Kidney, Heart)

Achievements

  • First hospital in India to perform a pediatric liver transplant successfully.
  • Accredited by JCI and NABH for clinical excellence.
  • Over 150,000 successful cardiac surgeries performed.
  • Advanced robotic surgeries across various specialties.
  • Recognized as one of the top hospitals globally for medical tourism.
