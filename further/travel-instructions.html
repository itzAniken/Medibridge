Travel Instructions for Clients Coming to India from the USA

Important Travel Guidelines for Your Trip to India

As you prepare for your surgery and stay in India, please follow these essential instructions to ensure a smooth journey:

  • Visa Requirements: Make sure to obtain your medical visa. MediBridge will assist you with the documentation process, and you'll receive the visa instructions within 24-48 hours.
  • Flight Arrangements: Ensure you book your flight in advance, preferably for a direct flight to Delhi International Airport (IGI). This will help you avoid long layovers and ensure timely arrival.
  • Health Precautions: Ensure that you have medical insurance coverage for your travel and treatment in India. Carry all necessary medical documents, including prescriptions, medical history, and test results.
  • Arrival in India: Upon arrival in India, you will be received by a representative from MediBridge at the airport. Please inform us of your flight details in advance for a smooth pick-up process.
  • Accommodation: You will be staying at one of the recommended hotels near Max Hospital, Delhi. The hotel staff will assist you with your check-in process, and you will be provided with all necessary details about the hospital visits.
  • Currency Exchange: It's advised to carry some Indian Rupees (INR) in cash for immediate expenses, although credit/debit cards are widely accepted in most places.
  • Safety and Health: Follow basic health and safety protocols, such as drinking bottled water and avoiding street food. Always carry your medication and doctor's contact information.
  • Medical Appointments: Your medical appointments and surgery will be coordinated by MediBridge. Please ensure you follow the medical schedule provided to you.

We are here to assist you every step of the way! If you have any further questions or need assistance, please feel free to contact our support team.
