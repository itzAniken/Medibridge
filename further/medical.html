Medical Report Requirements

What You Need to Upload

International patients are required to upload the following medical reports when filling out the form:

  • Recent Blood Test Reports
  • X-ray or MRI Scan Results (if applicable)
  • Medical History Summary (last 2 years)
  • Doctor's Prescription or Recommendations
  • Vaccination Records
×

Details About Required Medical Reports

To ensure accurate medical assessments, the following details are important:

  • Blood Test Reports: Includes CBC, cholesterol levels, and glucose tests.
  • Scan Results: X-rays, MRIs, or CT scans related to current conditions.
  • Medical History: Summary of diagnoses, treatments, and ongoing medications.
  • Prescription: Notes from your doctor explaining the need for the procedure or consultation.
  • Vaccination Records: Proof of immunizations for international travel.
