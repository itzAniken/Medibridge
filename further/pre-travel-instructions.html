Pre-Travel Instructions for International Patients

To ensure a smooth journey to India, please follow these guidelines:

  • Ensure that your passport is valid for at least six months from the date of travel.
  • Apply for a medical visa and carry all required documents, including the letter from MediBridge.
  • Pack any necessary medical records, prescriptions, and medications.
  • Check for vaccination requirements and COVID-19 regulations before your travel.
  • Arrange for currency exchange or an international debit/credit card for expenses.
  • Confirm your flight and accommodation bookings well in advance.
  • Carry copies of important documents, including visa, passport, and MediBridge confirmation letters.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact us at support@medibridge.com.
